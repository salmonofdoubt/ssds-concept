TRDA doctoral concept • André Baumann • v9.0

Stability-Aware Spatial Decision Support for Prioritising NbS Portfolios

A transferable decision method that distinguishes eligibility, suitability, priority, and decision robustness under uncertainty, using the Nanny-Delvin as a scientific demonstrator.

Overview Primary Data Stability Lab Timeline

Methodological Logic

Evidence Architecture

Combines official assessment inputs, spatial layers, and derived analytical variables into a governance-anchored stack.

Lexicographic Ranking

Nutrients first. Biodiversity connectivity, flood attenuation, and sediment reduction refine ties without flattening the score.

Stability Diagnostics

Testing claim persistence under data, parameter, structural, and scale uncertainty to classify robustness.

Primary Doctoral Data Generation

New empirical evidence generated specifically for this thesis to challenge and refine map-based assumptions through georeferenced field audits.

Reach-Audit Variables

Direct georeferenced observations of riparian width, bank poaching, livestock access, and field-to-stream connectivity.

Maintainability Proxies

Primary data on implementation persistence: recurrent stock pressure, repair burden, and access for upkeep.

Validation & Challenge

Empirical evidence for rule calibration and misclassification diagnosis, grounded in reach-audit campaigns.
